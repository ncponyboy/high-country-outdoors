Privacy Policy

High Country Outdoors
Last updated: March 2026

High Country Outdoors does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Location

The app requests your location only to sort trails by distance from your current position using the "Near Me" feature. Your location is never stored, transmitted, or shared with any third party.

Trail Data

Trail conditions are fetched from publicly available government sources including the National Park Service (NPS), US Forest Service (USFS), and state park agencies. No user data is involved in this process.

Purchases

Subscription purchases for High Country Outdoors Pro are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, store, or have access to any payment or billing information.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. No usage data is collected or transmitted.

Children

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
